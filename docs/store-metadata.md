Pick three. Add them up. Clear the deck.

Lucky 3 Solitaire is a fresh take on classic card games. Choose any three cards from the same column so their values sum to 9, 19, or 29 — and watch them vanish. Clear all 40 cards from the deck, or end with a single 3 in hand, and you win.

That's the whole rule. Takes 10 seconds to learn. Takes longer to put down.

**HOW TO PLAY**
A standard deck of 40 cards (Ace through 10, four suits) is dealt into columns. Tap three cards in the same column that add up to 9, 19, or 29. They disappear. Keep going until the board is clear — or until no moves remain.

**FEATURES**

Daily Challenge
A new puzzle every day, the same seed worldwide. Compete on the global leaderboard powered by Firebase. Come back tomorrow for a fresh board.

Fortune Poems

Card Back Collection
17 unique card back designs, unlocked as you play. From clean minimalist to rich material textures. Your deck, your style.

Combo System
Chain eliminations for bonus points. The board lights up as your streak builds. Satisfying in a way that never gets old.

18+ Achievements
Collect badges for speed runs, perfect clears, combo streaks, and quiet everyday milestones. No grinding required — just play naturally.

Fully Offline
No internet needed to play. No ads. No subscriptions. No account required. Everything — settings, scores, unlocks — lives on your device.

19 Languages
Interface and fortune poems available in English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Polish, Turkish, Arabic, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, and more.

**PRIVACY**
Lucky 3 collects nothing personal. Game state and settings are stored on-device with localStorage. Daily leaderboard scores are anonymous — no name, no email, no account. That's it.

Lucky 3 is a one-developer passion project. No venture funding. No dark patterns. Just a card game you can pick up for five minutes or an hour, without guilt.

Download free. Play forever.
