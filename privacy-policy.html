Privacy Policy

App: Lucky 3 Solitaire
Developer: Lucky3 Games
Effective Date: 2026-04-18

Overview

Lucky 3 Solitaire is a card puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data (if any) is collected when you use our app.

Data We Collect

Lucky 3 Solitaire collects no personal information.

  • No account required. You can play without registering or logging in.
  • No tracking or advertising. We do not use ad SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • No data sent to our servers. All game progress (scores, achievements, settings) is stored locally on your device using the browser's localStorage / device storage.

Daily Leaderboard (Optional)

If you choose to submit your Daily Challenge score to the leaderboard, your score and the current date are sent anonymously to Firebase (Google). No name, email, or device identifier is associated with the submission. This feature is entirely optional.

Device Permissions

  • Vibration (Haptics): Used for game feedback. No data is collected.
  • Internet: Used only for the optional leaderboard feature and loading the game on first launch.

Children

Lucky 3 Solitaire is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

The Daily Leaderboard feature uses Firebase (provided by Google). When you submit a score, it is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated effective date.
